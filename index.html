Volunteering At Agape

Welcome to volunteering for Agape Halloween. This Halloween party has been our annual tradition and celebration for the past 13 years, filling our beautiful home with hundreds of people to share in a wild experience.

This form is meant for those who want to volunteer to help make Halloween happen. We prioritize trusted friends, those who have financial difficulty purchasing a ticket, and those who want to directly participate in making the event wonderful.

If you are serious about volunteering for a 2-hour shift during the night, can make the commitment to showing up early, being on top of things and communicative, we would love to have you fill out the form below.

We're looking forward to seeing you there :)
