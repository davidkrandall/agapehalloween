Volunteering at Agape

Agape Halloween is an incredible experience, and we couldn't make it happen without the help of amazing volunteers like you! We have a limited number of discounted tickets available for those who are open to committing to a 2-hour shift during the event.

If you're interested in contributing to this unforgettable night, click the link below to sign up as a volunteer.

Sign Up To Volunteer

Once you've been approved, you'll receive an email with further details. At that point, you can use the button below to select a specific shift. The details of the shift duties are provided below.

Select a Shift (Once Approved)

Shift Duties

Door: You'll be checking tickets and helping to orient folks on where to enter and find coat check. It's key that you only let in those who have their names printed on the spreadsheet. Every year people try to weasel their way in, so by default, be a little suspicious. You'll also be in charge of putting stickers over their phone's cameras (with consent) and encouraging less phone time and more presence during the party.

Coat Check: Duties include checking coats, tagging coats with clothespin numbers, and handing out correlating ticket numbers. You'll be collecting and returning coats when guests bring their ticket number back. Coat check is located in the small room off the piano room, beside the kitchen.

Tea Lounge Support: Serving tea in the tea lounge and providing support to those who may be having a challenging time on substances. Volunteers are requested not to take substances in this room. For more information, see the Zendo Project.

Setup: Support includes lifting heavy objects, working with decorations, and supporting the transformation of the house.

Cleanup: Help put the house back together once the party is over, including lifting heavy objects.
