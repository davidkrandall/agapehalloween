Volunteering At Agape

Welcome to volunteering for Agape Halloween. This Halloween party has been our annual tradition and celebration for the past 13 years, filling our beautiful home with hundreds of people to share in a wild experience.

This form is meant for those who want to volunteer to help make Halloween happen. We prioritize trusted friends, those who have financial difficulty purchasing a ticket, and those who want to directly participate in making the event wonderful.

If you are serious about volunteering for a 2-hour shift during the night, can make the commitment to showing up early, being on top of things and communicative, we would love to have you fill out the form below.

We're looking forward to seeing you there :)

Shift Duties

Door
You'll be checking tickets and helping to orient folks on where to enter and find coat check. It's key that you only let in those who have their names printed on the spreadsheet. Every year people try to weasel their way in, so by default be a little suspicious.
You'll also be in charge of putting stickers over their phone's cameras (with consent) and encouraging less phone time and more presence during the party. Entrance will be located in the side yard.

Coat Check
Duties include checking coats, tagging coats with clothespin numbers and handing out correlating ticket numbers. Collecting guests' coats and returning them when they bring their ticket number back. We have a few extra people at this post at some hours and may ask you to take on another task or support other posts during your shift.
Coat check is located in the small room off the piano room, beside the kitchen.

Tea Lounge Support
Serving tea in the tea lounge and providing support to those who may be having a challenging time on substances. We request no one take substances in this room (including volunteers). More information on Zendo here: Zendo Project.

Setup
Support getting the house in order. This includes lifting heavy objects, working with decorations, and supporting the transformation of the house.

Cleanup
Support putting the house back together once the party is over. This includes lifting heavy objects.
